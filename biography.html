Bio

Hello, name is Jack Marshall. I was born on September 18th, 1993 and grew up around photography. My mother and father were both photographers, so photography to me is not only a way to capture a moment and express feelings, but a way of life. I went to college at ASU and graduated with a bachelors degree in Digital Photography. In my free time, I enjoy playing basketball with old friends, spending time with family, and watching tv with my dogs.

Why Weddings?

I chose to specialize in wedding photography mostly because I find weddings beautiful. It is what bonds two people for the rest of their lives. A wedding is a special day, and you want to capture those moments. One day you'll want to look back at the photos, whether its just randomly, or on a special occasion such as your anniversary. Regardless, a wedding is a special day, and you should want your photographer to be special, thats why you should choose me.
